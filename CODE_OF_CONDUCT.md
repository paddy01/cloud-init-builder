# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Cloud-Init Builder aims to be a practical, respectful project for people
building safer cloud-init configurations. We expect everyone who participates to
help keep the project welcoming, focused, and professional.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, reviews, and other
  project spaces.
- Give feedback on ideas and code, not on people.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and make room for different
  experience levels.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project and its users.
- Accept maintainers' decisions when they are made to protect project quality,
  scope, or community safety.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwanted attention.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make participation unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict
participation when behavior violates this Code of Conduct. Enforcement should be
proportionate to the impact and should prioritize the safety of project
participants.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact a project maintainer
privately when possible. If no private contact is available, open a public issue
asking for a private maintainer contact without including sensitive personal
details.

Reports should include enough context for maintainers to understand what
happened, such as links, screenshots, timestamps, and the people involved.
Maintainers should handle reports with discretion and avoid unnecessary public
disclosure.
